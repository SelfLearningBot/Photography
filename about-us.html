About Happiweds

Hi, my name is Rohit Poojari. I am a passionate photographer and visual storyteller, specializing in capturing timeless moments with creativity and precision. With years of experience in wedding, event, and fashion photography, I bring a unique perspective to every frame, ensuring each shot tells a compelling story.

At Happy Weds

We believe in turning emotions into memories, blending artistic vision with technical excellence to create stunning visuals that last a lifetime.

A little bit of my work.

I don’t just take photos—I create visual stories that capture emotions, energy, and moments that matter. Whether it's the raw emotion of a wedding, the vibrance of an event, or the power of a personal brand, my work is all about bringing your vision to life. With a blend of creativity, precision, and storytelling, I make sure every shot feels alive, cinematic, and unforgettable . From high-end fashion shoots to corporate branding and wedding celebrations, I focus on the details that turn ordinary shots into extraordinary memories
